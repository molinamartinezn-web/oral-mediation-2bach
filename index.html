2nd Bachillerato · English

Oral Mediation Practice

Practise your oral mediation skills with real-life situations.

🎯 Task

Your English-speaking friend is visiting your school and does not understand the information in a Spanish poster about healthy habits.

Explain the main ideas in English in a clear and simple way.

👣 Steps to prepare

  1. Read the information carefully.
  2. Select the most important ideas.
  3. Use your own words.
  4. Organise your answer.
  5. Speak clearly and confidently.

💬 Useful language

  • The poster explains that...
  • The main idea is...
  • It is important to...
  • In other words...
  • For example...
  • To sum up...

📖 Example situation

You read the following information in your school noticeboard about healthy habits.

HÁBITOS SALUDABLES

🍎 Come alimentos saludables 💧 Bebe agua a diario 🚶 Haz ejercicio regularmente 🌙 Duerme bien ☀️ Toma descansos

Mediation task: Explain this poster to your friend in English.

✅ Self-assessment

After your oral mediation, answer this question:

Did you explain the five main ideas from the poster?
